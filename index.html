LoungeLink — Design Gallery

25 iterations of a real-time airport lounge companion app

Priority Pass gives you access to airport lounges, but tells you nothing about what's happening inside — how crowded it is, what food is being served, or how long the wait is. LoungeLink is a concept for a real-time lounge companion that solves this. Below is the design journey from wild exploration to final refinement.
